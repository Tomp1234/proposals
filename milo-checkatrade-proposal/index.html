Security architecture

You asked about data ownership and how this compares to Gemini. Here's the direct answer.

🔒

Your data never leaves your environment

Milo connects to your data sources via read-only access. Queries are processed in a secure, isolated instance. No data is stored, cached or used for model training.

🏗️

Not a wrapper on a general-purpose LLM

Unlike Gemini or Copilot, Milo doesn't send your data to a shared model. Your instance is isolated. There's no cross-tenant data exposure, even under enterprise agreements.

🔑

Role-based access and audit trail

Users only see data they're authorised to see. Every query is logged. Your infosec team gets full visibility on what's being asked and by whom.

📋

Built for regulated and PE-backed environments

SOC 2 alignment, data residency controls, SSO integration. Designed for companies where "we'll look into it" isn't good enough for the board.
