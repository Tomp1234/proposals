The shape of the problem

Three questions multi-brand distributors can't answer quickly.

Every distributor we work with runs the same pattern you do. Own brands next to distributed brands, sold across marketplaces, grocery, prestige, and professional channels. The systems scaled. The visibility didn't.

01 / Sell-through

Sell-through is a week late.

You ship into dm, Rossmann, Douglas, Sephora, Zalando. The data comes back fragmented. Different formats, different cadences, different definitions of "week." By the time a velocity drop is visible, the reorder window has closed.

Latency
02 / Margin

True margin is guesswork.

Foamie margin at dm isn't the same number as Foamie on Amazon. Olaplex wholesale carries a different shape again. Promo funding, listing fees, returns, and freight all live in separate systems. Nobody owns the unified view.

Unified P&L
03 / Reporting

Country and brand views don't agree.

A Country GM asks "how's my market doing?" A Brand Manager asks "how's my brand doing?" Both pull from the same source, and neither report agrees. Monthly reporting turns into reconciliation work instead of decision work.

Brand, channel, country P&L. Net of everything.

The number is defensible because Milo cites the source rows. No hallucinations.

The obvious question

"We already use ChatGPT. What's different?" A lot, actually.

General LLMs are fine for drafting an email or summarising a PDF. They are the wrong tool for running a business across 18 brands and 80 countries.

ChatGPT (today)

General purpose
  • ○Knows everything on the internet. Knows nothing about New Flag.
  • ○Requires prompt-engineering skill to get useful answers.
  • ○Will confidently invent a margin number that isn't in any system.
  • ○No role-based access. Whatever the user types, goes.
  • ○Data handling varies by plan and region.
  • ○No memory of your brands, retailers, or country structure between questions.

Milo

Built for New Flag
  • ✦Connected to ERP, CRM, Shopify, marketplaces, retailer feeds, paid media, email.
  • ✦Knows your brands, SKUs, retailers, country entities, and margin structure.
  • ✦Can't hallucinate numbers. It only cites rows that exist in your systems.
  • ✦Role-based permissions per user, per brand, per market, per data subset.
  • ✦SOC 2 accredited. EU data residency (Frankfurt).
  • ✦Lives in Teams and WhatsApp. No new app to learn, no logins.
