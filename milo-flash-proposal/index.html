Example queries — Flash transaction environment
Business user asks

Why did airtime top-up volumes drop 18% in Gauteng last Tuesday?

Milo answers

Volume dropped across 340 merchants between 14:00 and 19:00. The drop correlates with a Vodacom network outage in that window — MTN top-ups in the same region were up 22% during the same period. No platform-side issue detected.

Business user asks

Which merchant tier is driving the most electricity voucher revenue this month, and how does that compare to last quarter?

Milo answers

Tier 2 merchants account for 48% of electricity voucher revenue this month, up from 39% last quarter. Tier 1 has dropped 6 points, driven by 3 large merchants reducing order frequency. Worth reviewing their account activity.

Business user asks

How is our DStv voucher product performing vs prepaid data across the Western Cape this quarter?

Milo answers

Prepaid data is outperforming DStv vouchers by 2.3x in the Western Cape, reversing the trend from Q3 last year. DStv volumes are flat but margins are higher. Prepaid data growth is concentrated in Cape Flats and Stellenbosch corridors.

Banking readiness query

Which customer segments show the most consistent transaction behaviour that would support a credit product?

Milo answers

3.2M customers show 8+ transactions/month with 12+ months of consistent history. Highest concentration in Gauteng and KZN. Electricity and prepaid data buyers are most regular — strong proxy for income stability. This segment is 4x more active than the median user.
