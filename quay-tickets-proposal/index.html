The Problem

Five Systems. One Organisation.

On any given Monday, understanding how the weekend performed means stitching together data from the ticketing platform, the ePOS system, Artefacts, SharePoint spreadsheets and finance. Each one managed by a different team. Each one formatted differently. It works. But by the time the picture is assembled, the moment to act on it has often passed.

  • The Monday morning question. Keith wants cross-system figures. That means someone building a spreadsheet. Every time. The answer exists, getting to it takes hours.
  • Forecasting in the dark. Monthly budgets are built from manual data pulls across multiple sources. There is no automated view of how revenue is trending across ticketing, events, food and beverage and retail.
  • The full picture of a show. A performance sells out. But what did it actually generate across ticket sales, bar spend and merchandise? Joining those numbers is a job in itself.
  • Visibility without a Power BI expert. The need for dashboards is real. Dashboards that are live, interactive and usable by everyone from Rachel down to the box office team, without needing someone to build and maintain them.
The Solution

What Milo Does

Milo connects to your existing data sources and lets anyone at The Lowry ask questions in plain English, getting answers with explanations. The why, not just the what.

No SQL. No pivot tables. No waiting for a report to be built. Type a question, get an answer that makes sense.

The key difference from Copilot: Milo only uses your data. It does not go to the web, does not share anything with Microsoft or OpenAI, and will not guess when it does not know. If your data does not contain the answer, Milo tells you that. It gives insight from what you have, nothing else.
